FormsPlayerController.uc
  1: Copy paste this class into your project that will hold the menu pages. 
  2: Then change the class it extends as you like.
  3: Make your game PlayerController extend the FormsPlayerController.
  4: Uncomment the Mainmenu and EscapeMenu archetypes and adjust to your needs.
  5: Run this on your UDKFrontEndMap and you will see your first Forms menu.
  6: Time to construct your pages and stylize them after using FStyle components.
  7: Images may be configured in UDKForms.ini or inside your FPage's code.
